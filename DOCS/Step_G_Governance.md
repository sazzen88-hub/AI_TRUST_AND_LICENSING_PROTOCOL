# Public Governance Model (High-Level)

ATLP governance consists of **three high-level groups** that guide the evolution of the protocol:

---

## 1. **Community Contributors**  
Open-source participants who:
- provide documentation updates  
- propose improvements  
- open issues  
- discuss ideas  
- contribute educational resources  

They help shape the public roadmap and support early adoption.

---

## 2. **Platform & Developer Stakeholders**  
Organizations and builders who integrate ATLP concepts into:
- generative AI  
- autonomous agents  
- content platforms  
- enterprise workflows  
- safety tools  

They provide practical feedback from real-world deployments.

---

## 3. **Stewardship Group (High-Level Only)**  
A coordinating body responsible for:
- reviewing major proposals  
- approving specification updates  
- producing public guidance  
- maintaining long-term protocol direction  

**NOTE:**  
Internal governance structures, validator rules, and authority roles remain private and part of the protected Master Specification.
